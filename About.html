RALPH LAUREN

History

Ralph Lauren is an iconic designer known for defining American style through his men’s and women’s fashions, home furnishings, accessories, and fragrances. Since its debut in 1967, the world of Ralph Lauren has come to convey a timeless luxury lifestyle with an enduring spirit of success, style, and a sense of togetherness. When Ralph Lauren Fragrances was launched in 1978, Mr. Lauren created an outstanding wardrobe of fragrances and grooming products for men and women that capture the brand’s iconic American perspective and timeless style. Today, the brand’s portfolio of best-selling fragrances includes scents that conjure a distinctive mood: The World of Polo, Ralph’s Club, Romance, Lauren, Safari for Men, Polo Sport, and our most sustainable fragrance yet—POLO EARTH, the next generation of Ralph Lauren Fragrances. As Mr. Lauren says, “Style is personal,” and Ralph Lauren Fragrances give the wearer yet another opportunity for personal expression through timeless style.

GIVING BACK IN STYLE

Of all his achievements, Mr. Lauren is especially proud of his multi-decade leadership in philanthropic causes that are near to his heart. Through personal contributions and fundraising, he has galvanized the fashion industry to raise millions of dollars toward a variety of causes, including cancer care and prevention and AIDS advocacy.
